Guacamole Recipe

Ingredients

  • 3-4 Avocados
  • 2 serranos peppers (or jalapenos)
  • 2 Roma tomatoes
  • 1 red onion
  • 1 bunch of cilantro
  • 3-4 limes

Steps:

  1. Cut avocados and mash in a mortar and pestle
  2. Dice Tomatoes
  3. Dice onion
  4. Wash and roll up the cilantro and chop
  5. Dice peppers
  6. Add ingredients into bowl and mix
  7. Cut limes and juice. Mix.
